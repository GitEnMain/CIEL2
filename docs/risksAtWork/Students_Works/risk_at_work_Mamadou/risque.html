Workplace Risks

5 common situations and how to protect yourself:

1. Office

Risk: Tripping over cables

Protection: Organize cables, clear walkways

2. Computer

Risk: Eye strain, back pain

Protection: Regular breaks, good lighting, proper posture

3. Garden

Risk: Sunburn, sharp tools

Protection: Sunscreen, hat, gloves

4. Construction Site

Risk: Falling objects

Protection: Wear a safety helmet

5. Factory

Risk: Hands caught in machinery

Protection: Never remove guards, use emergency stop

How to use this site

  • See risks: Click on "Home"
  • See solutions: Read the text in green
  • New risk? Contact your manager

In case of emergency, contact emergency services immediately.
