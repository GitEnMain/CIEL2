The 5 Major Risks

1. Falls Risks

Most common. The first cause of workplace accidents is falls. Wet floors, cluttered spaces, and working at heights are the main risks.

2. Psychosocial Risks

Work stress, burnout, harassment, or violence. Affects mental and physical health.

3. Chemical Risks

Being in contact with toxic substances, fumes, or dust can cause respiratory problems.

4. Electrical Risks

Exposed wiring, faulty equipment, or contact with water. Risk of shock or electrocution.

5. Fire Risks

Flammable materials, blocked exits, or equipment overheating. Requires clear evacuation plans.

The Golden Rule

When facing these situations, remember: Identify, Assess, Prevent. If you see something unsafe, report it immediately.
