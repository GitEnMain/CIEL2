Risks At Work

Slips and Trips

Wet floors and cluttered walkways are dangerous. Keep paths clear and clean up spills immediately.

Manual Handling

Incorrect lifting causes back injuries. Bend your knees, keep the back straight, and keep the load close.

Electrical Hazards

Avoid overloaded sockets and damaged cables. Only qualified personnel should repair electrical equipment.

Workplace Stress

High pressure can affect mental health. Take regular breaks and communicate with your manager.

Road Risk

Road accidents are a leading cause of fatalities. Respect speed limits and never use a phone while driving.
